Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how TT VDO DOWNLOADER ("we", "our", "us") handles information when you use our website.

1. What we collect

  • Anonymous usage analytics — a randomly generated user ID stored in your browser's localStorage, visit timestamps, the type of file you download (MP4 / MP3), and the TikTok URL you submitted.
  • Approximate location — your public IP address, country, city and ISP (collected via a third-party IP geolocation API).
  • Device info — browser user-agent, language and screen size.

We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal account information. We never ask you to sign up.

2. Why we collect it

This data is used solely to operate, secure, and improve the service — for example, to display total downloads, prevent abuse, and understand which countries our users come from.

3. Where we store it

Analytics data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore). Access is restricted to the site administrator.

4. Videos & files

We do not store, host, copy, or rebroadcast any TikTok videos. When you request a download, files stream directly from third-party servers to your device. We only log the fact that a download happened and which format was chosen.

5. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies or advertising trackers. We use localStorage for a single anonymous ID to count unique visitors.

6. Third parties

  • tikwm.com — fetches public TikTok video metadata.
  • ipwho.is / ipapi.co — looks up the country/city of your IP address.
  • Google Firebase — stores anonymous analytics.
  • Cloudflare / Vercel — hosting + CDN.

7. Your rights

Since we don't collect personal information, there is nothing personally identifiable to delete. You can clear your anonymous ID at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

8. Children

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.
